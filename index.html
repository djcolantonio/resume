About

From integrations to operations to AI-built software

I started as a Partner Integration Analyst at Cox Automotive, onboarding banks and building the API connections between lenders, dealers, and internal systems. It's where I learned how systems talk to each other, and how big businesses run.

I left to build something different: transitional living homes for people in recovery from addiction. Running it meant owning every piece myself — intake, case management, billing, compliance — and living with how much of it ran on spreadsheets, paper files, and manual processes that broke under real pressure.

So I started building my own tools. That grew into helping other small operators do the same — people who didn't want to overpay an agency, or didn't know where to start.
